Welcome to Wild Wonders, where you can experience the wonder of the animal kingdom up close!

Our zoo is home to over 54 animals from all over the world, including lions, tigers, elephants, gorillas, penguins, and more. We also have a variety of habitats, including a rainforest, a desert, and a savanna, so you can see animals in their natural environments.

In addition to seeing animals, our zoo also offers a variety of educational experiences, such as animal encounters, keeper talks, and behind-the-scenes tours. We also have a variety of family-friendly activities, such as a carousel, a playground, and a splash pad.

Whether you're looking for a fun and educational day out with the family or a chance to learn more about the animals of the world, Wild Wonders has something for everyone.

What to See and Do

Wild Wonders has something to offer everyone. Here are a few of our most popular attractions:

  • The African Savanna: See lions, giraffes, zebras, and other African animals up close.
  • The Asian Rainforest: Explore a lush rainforest and meet orangutans, tigers, and other Asian animals.
  • The Penguin World: Watch penguins swim and play in their icy habitat.
  • The Children's Zoo: Let your kids get up close and personal with farm animals and other small animals.
  • The Carousel: Take a ride on our classic carousel.
